BIO

I am a second-year PhD student with Kai-Wei Chang's group at UCLA.

Previously, I was a Pre-doctoral Young Investigator (PYI) at the Allen Institute for AI (AI2), where I worked with the PRIOR team on general purpose vision-language systems. Before that, I completed my MS in the Computer Science department at Stanford University, where I worked with Percy Liang's group on distribution shift in NLP.
